Last day at Clearwater

Today was our last day at Clearwater. We woke up early to help to work on community projects. Since we were taking pictures and videos for the website, we got to visit everyone and help everyone with their project. Some students were building a deck with the help of Mick. Some students helped paint signs and the restaurant. After finishing the community projects, we started preparing for the Community Feast. Students prepared many different types of dishes for the community. We made an Indian Vegan dish called “Aloo Matter.” We had the Community feast in the evening, everyone seemed to enjoy it a lot.

Ye Yuan
2nd Sept

Swan Lake Reserve

Today we visited the Swan Lake Indian Reserve. There we had a glanced at their culture. They showed us their traditional dances and learnt the significance of those dances. We met many aboriginal people there, and they had fascinating stories to share. Towards the end, we had a fascinating discussion about their dying language.

Munsif Aman
28th Aug

Getting to Clearwater

Today was our first day at clear water. We arrived at 830 to have pre-class discussion. We left for clear-water at 11:30am. It took us around 2 hours to get there. Once there, we started setting up our tents. After that we went to jeeane Berrad Garden to pick vegetables that we will need for rest of our stay. After picking vegetables we went to our camp-placed and were welcomed by Elder David Scott. He told us a brief history of indegenious people

Yihua Huang
